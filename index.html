The concept

Young people researching the questions that shape young lives.

Students are often asked to prepare for a future they have had little opportunity to investigate. Navio Journal will give student researchers a structured way to study that future and share what they discover.

The Director of Research will find and support student researchers who want to submit papers and articles. Their work will examine real changes affecting young people, using credible sources, clear reasoning, and language that students, families, and educators can understand.

The Journal will not tell every reader which path to choose. It will help them ask better questions, understand the evidence, and make more informed decisions.

Research and Navio's mission

Understanding change is part of finding direction.

01Investigate change

Student researchers examine forces reshaping education, work, technology, and community.

02Make it understandable

The Journal turns research into writing that young people can use, question, and discuss.

03Connect it to real choices

Readers gain context for courses, careers, skills, opportunities, and their next steps.

04Build informed direction

Better evidence helps youth approach the future with curiosity instead of guesswork.

“How will AI affect my job?”

This is the kind of future-facing question the Journal is being created to investigate, not answer with a slogan.

Our planned approach

Student-led, evidence-minded, and useful.

Navio Journal will give youth a serious place to contribute research while helping other young people understand issues that may influence their futures.

  • Student authorship
    Students will research and write the work submitted for consideration.
  • Research leadership
    The Director of Research will coordinate student researchers and the submission process.
  • Credible foundations
    Published work will be expected to identify sources and distinguish evidence from opinion.
  • Accessible communication
    Research should be readable and useful for students, parents, teachers, and counsellors.
  • Honest status
    No article, author, or result will be presented before it is real and ready.
